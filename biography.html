BIOGRAGPHY

I'm Eriana Gonzalez, a participant in the Road to Hire Tech Academy. I grew up in North Bergen, New Jersey before moving to Charlotte, North Carolina with the rest of my family, and I've lived here for the past 13 years. I love drawing, playing video games, reading, and listening to music. I've never done coding before joining the Road to Hire program, but I'm having a blast learning it.
